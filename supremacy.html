SUBWAY SURFERS

You can play the game forever, provided you don't mess up and hit an obstacle. Hitting an obstacle results in an immediate game over unless you have an item that lets you continue.Overall, Subway Surfers is a safe game for kids to play, especially when in-app purchases are not allowed and you don't connect the game to Facebook. We recommend parents play the game together with their children, and take advantage of the moment to talk to their kids about how online advertising works.
